Privacy Policy for Deen Routine
Effective Date: March 6. 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Deen Routine. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It
To provide accurate prayer times and local Islamic features, our app requires certain data:

Location Data (GPS): We request access to your device's background and foreground location. This is used strictly to determine your geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) and country code.

Purpose: This data allows the app to accurately calculate daily prayer times and determine the correct local calculation method (e.g., Diyanet, Egyptian General Authority of Survey, etc.).

Data Processing: Location data is processed locally on your device or sent to third-party prayer time APIs (Aladhan API at api.aladhan.com and Turkey's Diyanet API at awqatsalah.diyanet.gov.tr) solely for the purpose of fetching accurate prayer timings and Hijri calendar dates. We do not track your movements or store your location history on any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services
Our app may utilize third-party APIs and services to function properly. These services may collect information used to identify you, governed by their own privacy policies.

Location/Geocoding Services: OpenStreetMap Nominatim API (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) is used to search for cities and convert location names into coordinates for prayer time calculations. Your search queries are sent to their service.

Prayer Time APIs: 
- Aladhan API (api.aladhan.com) — Used to fetch prayer times and Hijri calendar dates based on your GPS coordinates
- Turkey's Diyanet API (awqatsalah.diyanet.gov.tr) — Used for official Turkish prayer times for specific cities

Quran Content API: AlQuran Cloud API (api.alquran.cloud) is used to fetch Quran verses and translations. This service does not process location data.

Azkar/Duas Content: Islamic supplications are loaded from a public GitHub repository (github.com/nawafalqubari/azkar-api). This service does not process location data.

4. Local Storage
We store your preferences (such as your chosen calculation method, saved Azkar, and notification settings) locally on your device. We do not upload this personal configuration data to our servers.

5. Permissions Required

Location: For astronomical calculations of prayer times.

Notifications/Alarms: To deliver Adhan alerts and reminders at the correct times.

6. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us your information. Since the core functionality of the app relies on local device calculations, we minimize data transmission to external servers. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

8. Contact Us
